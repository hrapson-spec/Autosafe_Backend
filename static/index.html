How this works

AutoSafe uses official UK DVSA MOT test data to calculate how likely different car models are to fail their MOT.

We analyse millions of real test results, grouped by:

  • Car make and model
  • Age of the vehicle
  • Mileage range

Data confidence indicates how much data we have for your specific combination:

  • High: 10,000+ tests
  • Good: 1,000-10,000 tests
  • Limited: Under 1,000 tests
