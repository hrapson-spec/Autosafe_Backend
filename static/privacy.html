Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Overview

AutoSafe is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data:

  • Search queries: Vehicle make, model, year, and mileage you enter (not stored permanently)
  • Basic analytics: Page views and general usage patterns (no personal identification)
  • Technical data: IP address and browser type for rate limiting and security

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No names, email addresses, or personal information
  • No vehicle registration numbers
  • No cookies for tracking (only essential technical cookies)

4. How We Use Data

Data is used solely to:

  • Provide risk estimates for the vehicles you search
  • Prevent abuse through rate limiting
  • Improve the service based on aggregate usage patterns

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties, except:

  • Our hosting provider (Railway) processes requests as part of service delivery
  • If required by law

6. Data Retention

Search queries are processed in real-time and not stored. Server logs are retained for up to 30 days for security purposes.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Since we collect minimal data without personal identifiers, there is typically no personal data to access.

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries, please use the feedback form on our main page.
