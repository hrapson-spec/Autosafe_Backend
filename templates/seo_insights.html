MOT Data Insights

We've analysed 142 million official DVSA MOT test records to uncover the data behind Britain's MOT results. These reports are updated regularly and free to cite with attribution.

Open data: All analysis uses official DVSA MOT test records published under the Open Government Licence v3.0. Journalists and researchers are welcome to cite these findings.

Important: MOT failure rates reflect test outcomes, not manufacturer quality. Many factors influence results including vehicle age, mileage, and maintenance history. See methodology on each report for details.
